About us

ABC Kids World entered the world of manufacturing educational products in the year 2011.

Thinking out of the Box and their creative smart work gave a Beautiful result. The company has its own reputation under creative educational aids.

The company makes a wide variety of products. Products like Balancing Beam, Hollow Tower, Learn 'N' Count and so on.

The Products are made in such a way that:

1. Child will be enthusiastic, strategic, and eager and use their creativity, imagination beyond the expectation levels.

2. Way of teaching to them in the way they feel easy and making them optimistic.

3. Child will have a gratifying experience and love what they are learning.

4. Help child to improve his concentration, co-ordination, independence and problem solving.

We genuinely believe in pure enhancement of life skills that will prepare your child for a lifelong love of learning. Children need products that have a purpose, durable, and are attractive. Quality over quantity is important when choosing Products. It shows respect for the children and their ability to respect their belongings.

All the products are manufactured under strict quality control and are child safe & user friendly. We are a socially aware, professional company that value children. We take pride in our products at ABC Kids World. We hope you and your children enjoy our selection of educational Products.

ABC Kids World would like to have interaction with the people in the field of education to develop new innovative ideas and products.
